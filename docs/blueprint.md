# **App Name**: Sales Territory Manager

## Core Features:

- Account Management: Add, edit, and manage sales accounts with details like account number, name, industry, and status (lead/customer).
- Contact Management: Associate contacts with accounts, including name, number, email, location, and main contact tag.
- Product Catalog: Maintain a global catalog of products with details like name, number, and available volumes (pails, drums, totes, bulk).
- Account-Specific Product Notes: Add notes related to specific products for individual accounts.
- Sales Insights: Use a generative AI tool to analyze sales data and notes to provide insights on account status and suggest potential actions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#63B5FF) for a professional and trustworthy feel.
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F4F8), almost white, to provide a clean and neutral canvas.
- Accent color: Teal (#46C1BE) for interactive elements, providing a vibrant but unobtrusive highlight.
- Font: 'Inter', sans-serif, for both headlines and body text, providing a modern and neutral reading experience.
- Use a set of consistent, minimalist icons to represent actions and data types, aligning with the overall clean aesthetic.
- Employ a clean and structured layout, prioritizing easy navigation and scannability of information. Mimic the use of whitespace and the panel-like feel shown in the image.
- Incorporate subtle animations for state transitions, such as loading indicators and confirmation messages.
